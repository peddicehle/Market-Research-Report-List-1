The market is categorized into oil-extended and non-oil extended types. Oil-extended E-SBR incorporates processing oils to enhance workability, lower production costs, and improve performance characteristics. In contrast, non-oil extended E-SBR is formulated without these oils, offering superior mechanical properties and thermal stability, making it suitable for high-performance applications.</p></p>
