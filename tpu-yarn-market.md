<p><p>The TPU yarn market is categorized into one-component and two-component types based on their composition and application. One-component TPU yarns are pre-formulated and ready for use, offering ease of processing and consistent performance. In contrast, two-component TPU yarns require mixing a resin and a hardener before application, allowing for customized properties and enhanced performance characteristics. Both types cater to various industries, including textiles and automotive, providing flexibility in product design and functionality.</p></p>
