<p><p>The rework solder flux market is categorized into three main types based on their formulation and activation level. Rosin flux is derived from natural resin and is widely used for its effective cleaning properties. Rosin Mildly Activated flux contains a small amount of activators for improved performance on oxidized surfaces while still being relatively gentle. Rosin Activated flux is more aggressive, featuring higher levels of activators that enhance soldering on challenging materials but may require more thorough cleaning post-application.</p></p>
