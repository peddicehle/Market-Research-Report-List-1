<p><p>The air leakage detector market comprises several types, including differential pressure type and direct pressure type detectors. Differential pressure detectors measure pressure differences between two points to identify air leaks, making them highly sensitive and suitable for various applications. Direct pressure type detectors, on the other hand, assess the absolute pressure within a given area to quantify leakage more straightforwardly. Other types may include thermal or ultrasonic detectors, which utilize different methodologies to detect air leaks, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
