<p><p>The glass paint market is segmented into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based glass paints are eco-friendly, low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs), and easy to clean with water, making them ideal for indoor applications. Solvent-based glass paints, on the other hand, offer superior durability, adhesion, and resistance to harsh weather conditions, making them suitable for outdoor use. Both types cater to diverse needs, with water-based paints emphasizing sustainability and solvent-based paints focusing on performance and longevity.</p></p>
