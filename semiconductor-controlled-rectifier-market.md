<p><p>The semiconductor controlled rectifier (SCR) market comprises two key types: unidirectional SCR modules and bidirectional SCR modules. Unidirectional SCR modules allow current flow in one direction, making them suitable for applications like power supplies and motor control. In contrast, bidirectional SCR modules permit current to flow in both directions, which is ideal for alternating current (AC) applications, such as phase control and light dimming. Both module types play vital roles in enhancing energy efficiency and enabling precise control in various industrial applications.</p></p>
