Additionally, the adoption of environmentally friendly and non-flammable flux removers is gaining traction due to stricter environmental regulations and safety standards.</p><p>Recent trends in the market include the development of bio-based and low-VOC (volatile organic compound) flux removers to meet sustainability goals.
