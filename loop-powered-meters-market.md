In pressure measurement, they gauge force exerted by fluids, while flow measurement devices track fluid movement rates. Level measurement sensors determine the height of substances in containers, and temperature measurement devices assess thermal levels. Other markets include specialized applications like humidity and pH measurement. These meters are favored for their simplicity and reliability in industrial settings.</p></p>
