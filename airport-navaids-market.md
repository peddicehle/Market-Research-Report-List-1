<p><p>The airport navigational aids (navaids) market includes various types of systems supporting aircraft navigation and landing. Non-Directional Beacons (NDB) and VHF Omnidirectional Ranges (VOR) serve as radio aids for navigation. Ground-Based Augmentation Systems (GBAS) enhance GPS accuracy for landings. Other emerging technologies and systems also contribute to improving navigational safety and efficiency in aviation.</p></p>
