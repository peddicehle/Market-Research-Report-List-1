Triple-wall sheets have three layers, providing enhanced insulation and rigidity, ideal for harsher climates. Other market types include multiwall sheets with more than three layers or specialized designs, catering to specific needs like higher impact resistance or improved light diffusion. These variations balance durability, insulation, and cost-effectiveness for diverse applications.</p></p>
