<p><ul><li>Direct TPMS</li><li>Indirect TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Automobile TPMS (Tire Pressure Monitoring System)** market is divided into **Direct TPMS** and **Indirect TPMS**. **Direct TPMS** uses sensors inside each tire to measure real-time pressure, providing accurate readings and alerts for underinflation. **Indirect TPMS** relies on wheel speed sensors and ABS systems to estimate pressure based on rotational differences, offering a cost-effective but less precise solution. Both systems enhance safety and fuel efficiency, with Direct TPMS being more advanced and Indirect TPMS being simpler and cheaper to implement.</p></p>
