<p><strong>The PTFE Architectural Membranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic PTFE Membrane</li><li>Hydrophilic PTFE Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE architectural membranes market is classified into two primary types: hydrophobic and hydrophilic PTFE membranes. Hydrophobic PTFE membranes repel water, providing excellent weather resistance and durability, making them ideal for outdoor applications. Conversely, hydrophilic PTFE membranes absorb moisture, enhancing breathability and vapor permeability, which is beneficial in applications requiring moisture control, such as in roofing and building facades. Both types offer unique properties suited for diverse architectural designs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
