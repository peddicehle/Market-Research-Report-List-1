<p><p>Drag finishing machines are advanced mechanical systems used primarily for surface treatment of various components. These machines work by dragging parts through a mixture of abrasive media, often in a high-speed rotating bowl or drum. Key applications include metalworking, automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing industries.</p><p>The Drag Finishing Machines Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.7% during the forecast period, driven by rising demand for high-precision surface finishing solutions.
