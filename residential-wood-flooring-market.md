<p><p>The residential wood flooring market is primarily divided into two types: single-layer and multi-layer wood flooring. Single-layer wood flooring consists of solid hardwood planks, typically offering durability and natural aesthetics. In contrast, multi-layer wood flooring features multiple layers, including a plywood or high-density fiberboard core topped with a hardwood veneer, providing enhanced stability and resistance to moisture. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and environmental conditions, influencing installation methods and overall performance in residential spaces.</p></p>
