<p><strong>The Portable Water Flosser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Frequency</li><li>Frequency Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable water flosser market can be categorized into two main types: constant frequency and frequency conversion. Constant frequency models operate at a steady pulsation rate, providing consistent cleaning pressure, making them easy to use and effective for daily dental care. Frequency conversion models, on the other hand, offer varying pulse rates, allowing users to customize their flossing experience for more intensive cleaning or gentle care as needed. This adaptability can cater to different user preferences and dental requirements.</p></p>
