<p><p>Teflon tape, used for sealing pipe threads, comes in various densities to suit different applications. Low-density tape is thin and easy to tear, ideal for small, low-pressure pipes. Standard density is versatile, commonly used for general plumbing tasks. Medium-density tape offers better durability and sealing for moderate pressure systems. High-density tape is thicker and robust, designed for high-pressure or industrial applications. Each type ensures leak-free connections, with density determining strength and suitability for specific pressure and temperature conditions.</p></p>
