<p><p>Wireless cycle computers are categorized into two main market types: analog and smart. Smart cycle computers, on the other hand, integrate advanced features such as GPS, smartphone connectivity, and app integration, offering real-time data analysis and route planning. These cater to tech-savvy cyclists seeking enhanced performance tracking and a more connected riding experience.</p></p>
