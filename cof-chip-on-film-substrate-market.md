<p><p>The COF (Chip On Film) substrate market primarily consists of single-layer and double-layer types. Single-layer COF substrates offer a more straightforward and cost-effective solution for bonding chips to flexible circuits, making them suitable for simpler applications. In contrast, double-layer COF substrates provide enhanced performance, such as better electrical characteristics and improved thermal management, making them ideal for more complex devices. Each type caters to different needs depending on the application requirements, performance criteria, and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
