<p><p>The 3D printed food market comprises various technologies: </p><p>1. **Extrusion-based Printing** utilizes food pastes or purees, layering them to create textured foods, ideal for custom shapes and flavors. </p><p>2. **Selective Laser Sintering and Binder Jetting** involve binding powdered ingredients, allowing for intricate designs and complex structures not possible with traditional methods. </p><p>3. **Inkjet Printing** employs food-safe inks, enabling precise placement of flavors and colors, perfect for enhancing visual appeal and customizing dishes. Each type offers unique advantages for creativity and customization in culinary applications.</p></p>
