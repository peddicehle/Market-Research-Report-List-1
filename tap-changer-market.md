<p><strong>The Tap Changer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OLTC</li><li>DETC/NLTC/OCTC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tap changers are essential devices in power transformers, regulating voltage levels to maintain supply quality. The market includes several types: </p><p>1. **OLTC (On-Load Tap Changer)** adjusts voltage while the transformer is operational, ensuring stable performance.</p><p>2. **DETC (De-Energized Tap Changer)** operates when the transformer is offline, allowing for maintenance.</p><p>3. **NLTC (No-Load Tap Changer)** functions similarly to DETC but offers more flexibility in settings during downtime.</p><p>4. **OCTC (Off-Load Tap Changer)** is used in specific applications where load variation is minimal, providing basic voltage adjustment.</p></p>
