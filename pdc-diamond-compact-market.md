<p><ul><li>HPHT</li><li>CVD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PDC Diamond Compact markets are primarily categorized into two types: HPHT (High Pressure High Temperature) and CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition). HPHT diamonds are synthesized by mimicking natural geological processes, resulting in high-quality diamonds used for industrial applications. Conversely, CVD diamonds are developed through a chemical process that allows for greater control over properties, creating higher purity and clarity. Both methods cater to diverse applications in cutting, drilling, and mining, addressing specific industry needs while advancing diamond technology.</p></p>
