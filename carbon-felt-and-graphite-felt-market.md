<p><p>Carbon felt and graphite felt are high-performance materials made from carbon fibers, processed into a flexible, porous, and lightweight structure. Carbon felt is primarily composed of amorphous carbon, while graphite felt undergoes additional high-temperature treatment to achieve a more ordered graphite structure. Both materials exhibit excellent thermal conductivity, chemical resistance, and high-temperature stability, making them ideal for applications in industries such as energy storage, aerospace, metallurgy, and electronics. They are widely used in fuel cells, batteries, thermal insulation, and filtration systems.</p><p>The Carbon Felt and Graphite Felt Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.8% during the forecast period.
