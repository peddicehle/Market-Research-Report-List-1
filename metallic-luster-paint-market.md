<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metallic luster paint, known for its reflective, shimmering finish, is available in two primary market types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based paints use organic solvents as carriers, offering durability, smooth application, and high gloss, but emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Water-based paints use water as a carrier, providing eco-friendliness, low VOC emissions, and easier cleanup, though they may require more coats for similar effects. Both types cater to automotive, industrial, and decorative applications, balancing performance and environmental considerations.</p></p>
